Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about shopping, shipping, and our fashion services.

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $9.99
  • Next Day Delivery: $19.99 (order by 2 PM)

International shipping is available to over 45 countries. Delivery times vary by location.

We want you to love your purchase! Here's our return policy:

  • 30-day return window from delivery date
  • Free returns with prepaid shipping label
  • Items must be unworn with original tags attached
  • Full refund processed within 5-7 business days

Exchanges are also available for different sizes or colors of the same item.

Finding the perfect fit is easy with our sizing tools:

  • Size Guide: Detailed measurements for each product
  • Fit Predictor: AI-powered sizing recommendations
  • Customer Reviews: See how items fit on different body types
  • Virtual Styling: Book a free consultation with our stylists

Still unsure? Our customer service team is happy to help with personalized sizing advice.

  • Special Occasion Styling: Perfect outfits for weddings, events, and more

We accept all major payment methods for your convenience:
